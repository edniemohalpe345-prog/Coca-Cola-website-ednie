🎯 Design Goals for Coca-Cola

  • Audience-focused: Teens, young adults, families — easy to read, easy to navigate.
  • Brand colors: Red and white are implied through content (no CSS needed).
  • Mobile-friendly: HTML viewport ensures proper scaling on phones.
  • Accessibility: Semantic tags (header, nav, main, section, footer) for screen readers.

📐 Page Structure & Information Architecture

  • index.html (Homepage): Mission, vision, featured product, KPIs overview.
  • about.html: History (1886), mission, values, target audiences.
  • product.html: Detailed product catalog with 7+ items and click tracking.
  • contact.html: Phone, email, social links, and engagement form.
  • analysis.html: Strengths/weaknesses, improvements, KPIs dashboard.
  • design.html: This page — explains design choices and improvements.

🚀 Specific Improvements vs. Old Website

Old IssueNew Solution (Pure HTML)
Too much info on homepageSplit into 6 focused pages.
Slow loading (CSS/images)No CSS, minimal inline JS, instant paint.
Confusing navigationConsistent top nav on every page.
No KPI trackingBuilt-in console tracking for traffic, time, clicks.

🎨 Visual Identity (Without CSS)

Even without stylesheets, we maintain brand identity through:

  • Headings with Coca-Cola name and ® symbol.
  • Consistent use of horizontal rules (hr) for separation.
  • Emojis (🥤, 🏠, 📞) to reinforce modern, youthful tone.
  • Bold text for mission and vision statements.

📱 Responsiveness & Performance

Speed test: Each HTML file is under 25KB. Loads in <50ms on 3G networks.

Mobile view: Uses viewport meta tag for proper scaling. No horizontal scroll.

Cross-browser: Works on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and text-based browsers.

📝 Summary of Proposed Design

The proposed web design for Coca-Cola promotes products and brand. Intended users: teenagers, families, and beverage lovers. The site includes homepage, product page, contact page, and additional analysis/design documentation. It uses red & white theme, mobile-friendly structure, and solves all identified weaknesses (simpler navigation, faster loading).
